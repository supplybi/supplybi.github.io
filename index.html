Analyse

Analyse your inventory profile, identify slow moving and excess stock, devise inventory policies, plan purchase orders, manage inventory levels and drive down working capital.

Simulate

Simulate inventory consumption using a monte carlo simulation. Test the results of analytical models and identify problems with safety stock allocations, or forecast models and take preemptive action.

Explore

Explore your supply chain data using the computational power of Python and the many existing data science and analysis tool.

Features

  • Inventory Analysis
    • Safety Stock
    • Demand Variability
    • Economic Order Quantity
    • Reorder Level
    • Reorder Quantities
  • Monte Carlo Simulation
    • Simulate, summarise, analyse the transactions
    • compare analytical calculations vs simulation
